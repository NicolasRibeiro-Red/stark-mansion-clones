# Epigraph

Paginas: 12-12


If a factory is torn down but the rationality which produced it is left
standing, then that rationality will simply produce another factory. If
a revolution destroys a government, but the systematic patterns of
thought that produced that government are left intact, then those
patterns will repeat themselves.… There’s so much talk about the
system. And so little understanding.
—ROBERT PIRSIG, Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance
