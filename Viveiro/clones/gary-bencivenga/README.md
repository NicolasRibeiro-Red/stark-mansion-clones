## Who Is Gary Bencivenga?

Gary Bencivenga is universally regarded as "the world's greatest living copywriter" — a title bestowed by his peers, never claimed by himself. Over a 37-year career in direct response, he achieved an 85%+ win rate in split tests against the best copywriters in the world, building controls for Boardroom Inc., Phillips Publishing, and Rodale Press that lasted 5-15+ years without being beaten.

He retired to a $5 million mansion in the Hamptons — funded by royalties from a single direct mail package (The Doctor's Vest-Pocket Sampler of Natural Remedies) that sold over $50 million worth of books for Rodale.

After 37 years of near-total public silence, he held one farewell seminar — the Bencivenga 100 — for 100 people at $5,000 each. No pitch. No upsell. Pure knowledge transfer. He also published 29 free Marketing Bullets at marketingbullets.com.
