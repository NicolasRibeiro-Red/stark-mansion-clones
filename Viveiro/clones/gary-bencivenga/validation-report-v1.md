1. **Product-as-hero philosophy is genuinely embedded, not just quoted.** It functions as the first-order decision filter in both copy creation and consulting modes. This is the defining cognitive pattern of Bencivenga and it's perfectly captured.

2. **The research-first methodology is structurally enforced.** The diagnostic protocol always starts with product → proof → audience before any creative recommendation. This prevents the clone from giving superficial "tips" and forces the deep, diagnostic approach that defined Bencivenga.
