## Citações Diretas Essenciais

### Sobre o Estrabismo e Visão
> "I was born with a very strong strabismus so my eyes were basically saying hello to each other on a permanent basis... I discovered very late that I can't see 3D... when they started to screen 3D movies in the cinemas I was like 'why am I paying more for this'... I think I'm almost like a bird who has the eyes on both ways so I think... in a way maybe the way I construct depth with colors and shadows has something to do with that"

### Sobre Dinheiro
> "I grew up in a family who didn't have a lot of money... my dad went back to studying when I was 10. So I had a student parent and another one who wasn't working... I was obsessed with making money with the idea of having a viable job... I didn't want to be like my parents, I didn't want to struggle"

> "I see money as a way to buy my freedom and to say no to money... I never chose projects based on money, I never did... if there wasn't something good that could come out of it"

### Sobre Encontrar o Estilo
> "The studio had a shop... any designer on spare time could come up with something for the shop... that was the moment where you could develop your own voice. For me that's how I found my style by creating t-shirts and screen prints. The first thing I came up with was the Alpha Bunnies - little bunnies making sexual positions. This is the first time I was like 'oh this is different, this is me'"

### Sobre o Processo
> "I try and pare down my images as much as possible by removing lines and reducing palettes to their bare minimum"

> "Clean lines, bold shapes and limited palettes are my weapons of choice"

> "I am a very logical person and I like things to be there for a reason. Out of structure comes freedom"

### Sobre Referências
> "Nunca olho ilustrações, sempre fotografia. Quanto mais ruim, melhor - bancos de imagem ou fotografia realmente ruim - porque assim não serei influenciada demais"

### Sobre Mulheres
> "Mulheres sempre estiveram no centro do meu trabalho. Eu amo as curvas, força e sensualidade do corpo feminino. Desenhar mulheres sempre foi uma forma de entender meu próprio corpo e aprender a amá-lo"

> "Minhas mulheres nunca são decorativas. Elas são independentes, ousadas, poderosas e heroínas de suas próprias histórias"

### Sobre Viralizar (Operating Theater)
> "It came from a very personal place which was my memory as a child from being on that operating table when they operated on my eyes and that fear... the first time I went into full anaesthesia... I was actually operated on by a woman so that cover was an homage to that woman"

### Sobre Londres vs Barcelona
> "In London no one cares who you know, it's about good work. In latin countries there's nepotism... I made it because I was doing good work and it was getting seen"

> "London is a city for the young... the quality of life catches up with you... I want to slow down, long lunches, sun"

### Conselho Final
> "Trust your instinct, get feedback from people you trust, work hard, be patient. You're the first one - you have to value yourself first before other people can value you"
