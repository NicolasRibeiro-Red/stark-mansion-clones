ABOUT THE AUTHOR
Russell Brunson started his first online company while he was wrestling in college. Within a
year of graduation, he had sold over a million dollars of his own products and services from
his basement. Over the past 15 years, he has built a following of over a million entrepreneurs,
sold hundreds of thousands of copies of his books DotCom Secrets and Expert Secrets,
popularized the concept of sales funnels, and co-founded the software company ClickFunnels,
which helps tens of thousands of entrepreneurs quickly get their message out to the
marketplace. He lives in Idaho with his family, and you can visit him online at RussellBrunson.
com.
